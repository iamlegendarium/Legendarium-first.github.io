About Us

Leading the industry

Diamond Offshore Drilling. (IDL) is a leading Irish Mineral/Oil Petroleum  Exploration and Ground Investigation Contractor and has carried out major geotechnical investigation and mineral/Oil Petroleum  exploration drilling contracts in Ireland, the UK, France and the Isle of Man. Our contract work is strictly carried out with the aim to provide a comprehensive and quality service to our clients within budget and on schedule.

Fieldwork Operations

25 Operational Rigs (Cable Percussive and Rotary); 20 Rotary Core Rigs (tracked & tractor mounted); CS14, DeltaBase 520, GT1100 Drill Rigs; 5 Dando 2000 Cable Percussive Boring Rigs; 3 Geotool DPH Rigs; 1 Tracked Excavator; Probing (Peat, Macintosh, Cobra); In-Situ Sampling and Testing; 4 Senior Management; 6 Engineers / Geologists; 4 Laboratory Technicians; 18 Senior Drillers / Drillers; 4 Mechanics / Fitters

IDL has its own rock and soils laboratory facilities where testing is carried out in accordance with BS1377, BS 812, ISRM and other relevant standards.

  • Cable Percussive Boring (150mm, 200, 250mm)
  • Rotary Core Drilling (AQ,BQ,NQ,HQ,PQ,GeoboreS)
  • Rotary Percussive Drilling
  • Navi Directional Drilling.
  • Trial Pit & Slit Trench Excavation and Logging
  • Dynamic Probing (DPH,DPSH)
  • Cobra & Macintosh probing / Window Sampling
  • Russian Sampling
  • Standpipe Installation (Water, Gas, Data Loggers)
  • Pump Tests, Soil Infiltration Tests
  • Permeability Tests (Variable Head, Packer)
  • Borehole Logging (Soil & Rock)
  • Core orientation, Discontinuity / fracture mapping
  • Borehole Surveys (Single, Multishot)
  • In-Situ Tests (Plate Tests, CBR, Shear Vane)
  • Topographical Surveying (GPS)
  • Inclinometer Installation and Monitoring
  • Static Cone Penetration Tests
  • Vibrating Wire and Pneumatic Piezometers
